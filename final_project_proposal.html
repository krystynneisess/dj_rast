Title: DJ Rast

Summary: DJ Rast is a photo-manipulation program which reacts to audio input. Specifically, we will be mapping qualities such as frequency and amplitude patterns from MP3 files to various rasterization and SVG transformations. Through DJ Rast, we hope to explore and visualize the interrelationship between said audio and visual features.

Team Members: Krystyn Neisess, Huu Tinh Pham, Chase Porter

What you've envisioned is DJ Rast, a photo-manipulation program which responds to audio input, as opposed to more traditional, explicit user input (eg. manually selecting individual filters). With an appreciation for both sight and sound as universal mediums of communication, we hope to explore the relationships that manifest when mapping auditory qualities--namely, frequency (pitch) and amplitude (volume)--with various image filters and transformations.
